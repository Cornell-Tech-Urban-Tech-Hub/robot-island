# Airport Automation
The John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK) is piloting the use of autonomous electric shuttles to augment the existing AirTrain connecting the outer parking lots to Terminal 4. 

## Integration Challenges
This case explores the regulatory hurdles of operating autonomous vehicles within FAA jurisdictions and the complex V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) communication required to interact safely with pedestrian crosswalks and traditional taxi dispatch zones.

- **Scale:** 12 automated shuttles
- **Route:** 1.5 miles
- **Status:** Pilot phase
