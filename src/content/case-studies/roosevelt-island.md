# Micro-Transit Alternatives
Given Roosevelt Island's geographically constrained, single-major-artery design, it represents an ideal "walled garden" for early autonomous transit adoption. This study evaluates replacing the aging Red Bus fleet with a continuous loop of multi-passenger autonomous pods.

## Cornell Tech Campus Integration
The proposed loop would connect the subway station, the tramway, and seamlessly integrate into the pedestrian-heavy zone of the Cornell Tech campus at the island's southern end.

- **Scale:** 8 Pods
- **Route:** 2.0 miles
- **Status:** Conceptual Planning
